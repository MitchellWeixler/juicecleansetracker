Day 4+ reintroduction plan
Your digestive system has been resting for 3 days. Reintroduce food slowly — rushing back to solid meals will make you feel awful and undo much of the benefit.
Foods to avoid for 48 hours after the cleanse
🚫 Red meat, fried food, or fast food
🚫 Alcohol or caffeine (go gentle for 1–2 days)
🚫 Processed sugar, pastries, candy
🚫 Heavy dairy (hard cheese, ice cream)
🚫 Raw cruciferous veg in large amounts (can cause bloat)
Signs the reintroduction is going well
✅ Digestion feels smooth and regular
✅ No bloating or cramping after meals
✅ Energy stays stable through the day
✅ Cravings for sugar and processed food feel reduced
